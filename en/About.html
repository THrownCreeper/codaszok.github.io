The Story of Codászok

Our little story begins there when, as an eighth-grader, we decided we want to study in the field of informatics. All of us have the interest to be work somewhere where informatics will be useful knowledge. When the school informed us about we were admitted, we were one step closer to the final goal. NEU set us the task of making projects throughout the year. The first was the introduction project, in which we introduce our team, our team name, and ourselves. We wanted our team name to be related to IT, so we became Codas (Code + Ászok is a Hungarian word game, in English, it means Code Aces).

Common points:

  • All three of us want to graduate and have an advanced language exam 
  • We love cycling
  • We are all beginner content makers
  • We hate pineapple pizza
  • We live a life free of addictions and harmful substances

Differences

  • Everyone is good in other thing, and that makes our team distinctly strong and versatile
  • Our way of thinking is most different
  • Our values are different
  • We want to take a different job in life
